Description:
In the quiet stillness of a peaceful town, one student's journey home takes a dark turn as reality begins to fracture.
"Fragments of Reality" is a psychological horror experience that explores the unsettling power of stress and mental instability.

You play as a college student returning home for the summer break.
Burdened by the weight of academic pressures, you arrive at your house to find it empty—your mother is away. But as you unlock the door and step inside, something is terribly wrong.
What seems like a routine return to a familiar place slowly twists into a nightmarish descent into madness.

As you move through the house, you’ll experience vivid hallucinations and unsettling shifts in the environment.
The boundary between reality and delusion becomes increasingly difficult to decipher as you uncover distorted memories, conflicting perceptions, and hidden truths.
Each step you take in the game pulls you deeper into the disorienting maze of your mind.

Can you uncover the truth before it consumes you, or are you trapped in a cycle of your own fractured reality?

Description (Revised):
Return to a place you once called home... only to find it no longer feels familiar.

In "Fragments of Reality," you take on the role of a college student returning home after a stressful year.
What should be a peaceful summer break quickly unravels as your perception of reality begins to falter.
Alone in an empty house, you must navigate through unsettling moments, distorted memories, and strange occurrences that blur the line between what's real and what's imagined.

As you explore the house, nothing is as it seems.
The familiar environment shifts unexpectedly, and the deeper you go, the more your mind becomes a maze of confusion.
Is it the weight of academic pressure weighing you down? Or is something darker at play?

Every corner hides a secret. Every noise could be a warning. Can you trust your own mind, or has it betrayed you?
